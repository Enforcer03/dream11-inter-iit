1. Press the START button to initialise the game.
2. You can watch the Video Demo if you need any help.
3. First input the date of the match. For test matches input the date the test started
4. Select the league of the match from the available options and then select the relevant match and click NEXT.
5. Select atleast 11 players from both the teams by clicking on the player cards. 
6. If you want to remove some player from the team, just click on the player in the SELECTED PLAYERS section.
7. After selecting players from both the teams, click on PREDICT 11 and wait for the model to give the predicted team with the most fantasy points
8. Now you will see the Predicted 11 from the model. You click on individual player cards to get information why they were taken in the team.
9. You can also click on Get Team Info to get the team summary.
10. Now you can click on SWAP to replace any player with other and see in real-time the changes it would make to the team.
11. Click FINALIZE to get the final team after swapping. You can click SAVE to save the team as screenshot.
